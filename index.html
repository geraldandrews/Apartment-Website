Welcome to The Northwinds!

The Northwinds is an affordable housing community located in Wytheville, Virginia. It was built in 1978 and includes a total of 144 units which consists of 16 townhomes in 3 distinct buildings, 8 two-story garden-style buildings, and one community center which includes a community and events room and the office of The Northwinds. The property sits within a nice rural mountain setting and provides it's residents with beautiful, safe, and affordable housing.

The Northwinds community is also conveniently located just minutes away from shopping centers, recreation centers, restaurants, and medical facilities located in and around downtown Wytheville. It is served by the Wythe County Public Schools attendence zone and is located near all of the public schools and Wytheville Community College in Wytheville.

At the Northwinds, we offer spacious 1, 2, 3, and 4 bedroom apartment homes that were built and designed for your comfort and convenience. In addition to our great floor plan options and maintenance free living, our community provides a wide selection of amenities such as convenient on-site parking options, a clubhouse, balconies, and more. At The Northwinds you are right at home.

Call us today to make an appointment to see for yourself why The Northwinds should be your new home. We look forward to hearing from you!
